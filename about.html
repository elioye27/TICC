About Us

Our Mission

The vision of the Church Trinity International Christian Center is to present Jesus Christ God's Son, to every person in every culture and nation as Savior, The Baptizer with the Holy Spirit. Since the founding of Trinity International Christian Center﻿﻿ ﻿the vision has been realized through evangelism, community involvement and missionary work at the local congregation in the United States and abroad. This has been also achieved through the nurturing and training of believers into leadership on all levels of church life, and in cooperation with other members of the body of Christ at large in the fellowship of the Gospel and the fulfillment of the biblical mandate to be "light" and "salt" on the Earth.

Our Assignemnt

Dedicated to developing a healthy growing church, and our commitment is to plant churches around the world led by loving servants of Jesus Christ.
